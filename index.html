General

Triathlon is an athletic contest consisting of three different events, typically swimming, cycling, and long-distance running. So it's a really complete sport.

Trainings

Each training is based on one discipline. An athlete needs to train a lot in a week. They are essentially based on long distant trainings (middle background for example).

Competition

There are differents distances depending on the different competitions. For the youngest athletes, it is size S, while it's way longer for adults (size L). The biggest is the Iron Man: this one is for the bravest athletes.

My personal experience

I've been training for 5 years in the Poissy Triathlon club. I did a lot of competition, and I was really good. I have one france championship gold medal, and two silver medals.
